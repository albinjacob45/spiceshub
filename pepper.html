Premium Black Pepper

Harvested from climbing vines in the lush forests of Wayanad and Idukki, our Black Pepper is a powerhouse of bold flavour and medicinal potency. Variety Malabar Black Pepper
Grade Export Grade · FAQ / MG1

Aroma Profile

Bold, woody, and intensely pungent with warm spice notes, earthy undertones, and a clean, sharp heat that builds on the palate. High piperine content ensures medicinal-grade potency.
